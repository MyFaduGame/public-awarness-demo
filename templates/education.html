⚠️ What You Just Experienced

You granted permissions to an app and it accessed your real contacts and photos. This is exactly what malicious apps do!

🔐 1. Always Verify QR Codes

  • Don't scan random QR codes: They can lead to malicious websites
  • Check the URL: Before entering any information, verify the website address
  • Use a QR scanner with preview: See the URL before opening it

📱 2. App Permissions - Be Selective

  • Grant minimum permissions: Only give apps the permissions they need
  • Review permissions regularly: Check which apps have access to your data
  • Contacts and photos are sensitive: Think twice before granting these permissions
  • Location data is dangerous: Only share when absolutely necessary

🛡️ 3. Protect Your Personal Data

  • Use strong passwords: Different password for each account
  • Enable 2FA: Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security
  • Be careful what you share: Think before posting personal information online
  • Regular backups: Keep your important data backed up securely

💡 Pro Tips for Cybersecurity

✅ Use a password manager
✅ Keep your apps and OS updated
✅ Use a VPN on public WiFi
✅ Be skeptical of unsolicited requests
✅ Check app reviews before installing
✅ Monitor your accounts for suspicious activity

📊 Real Statistics

  • 93% of data breaches involve personal information
  • 85% of apps request unnecessary permissions
  • 60% of people don't read permission requests
  • 1 in 3 users has been a victim of data theft
