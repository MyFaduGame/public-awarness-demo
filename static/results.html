🔒 What Websites CANNOT Automatically Access

Modern browsers protect your privacy!

Without your explicit permission, websites CANNOT access:

  • Your phone number
  • Your contacts list
  • Your SMS or text messages
  • Your WhatsApp or messaging app data
  • Your photos or camera roll
  • Your files or documents
  • Your installed applications
  • Your microphone or camera (without permission)
  • Your precise GPS location (without permission)

These features require explicit user permission through browser prompts.
